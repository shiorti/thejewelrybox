Our Family History

Our history begins with George Chiappa, the son of Italian immigrants from Lake Como, Italy. Born and raised in New York in the 1920s, he made his mark in the jewelry and watch trade. When his firstborn daughter, Theresa Chiappa, showed interest in the trade, he was excited to pass along his knowledge and worked with her for the decades they had together until his passing in 1984.

Theresa has since taken the torch and carried on the family legacy of quality and beauty. Her beautiful store in the hills of New Jersey has been a gem in the community for 40 years.

When I was just 12, my aunt introduced me to her world. Throughout my teens, she trained me just as her father had trained her, and in my 20s, I moved from my home in Boulder, Colorado to be nearer to her and began getting involved in other aspects of the trade, working with antiques as well as diamonds and fine jewelry, honing my skills as a jeweler.

Together, we have taken our boutique shoppe online! We hope to share our love of beautiful jewelry with you, and strive to provide superior customer service with pieces we can proudly stand behind.
